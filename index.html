About ENG MUTEVU

Who I Am

A proud son of Kilome in Makueni County, Hon. Eng. Mutevu has steadily risen through Kenya's political landscape. He holds a Master’s degree in Public Policy from Kenyatta University. Before venturing into politics, he founded "Tujenge Vijana Kilome", a community-based organization that empowered over 5,000 youth with vocational and agribusiness skills. His transformative "Matatu Bora Initiative" improved rural transport by introducing safer, regulated PSV services across Kilome and surrounding wards.

Development Record

Built 12 new ECDE centers

Upgraded 3 health centers to 24-hour facilities

Installed free WiFi in 5 markets

Tarmacked 8km of access roads

Political Journey

  • 2022-Present: MP for Starehe Constituency
  • 2017-2022: Chair, Parliamentary Committee on Finance
  • 2013-2017: MCA Kamukunji Ward, Nairobi
  • 2010-2013: Youth Representative, Constitutional Implementation Commission

Core Values

  • Integrity: Returned 37% of office budget to Treasury in 2021
  • Inclusivity: Pioneered sign language in constituency meetings
  • Innovation: Launched Kenya's first e-CDF system
  • Service: Holds monthly public barazas in all wards

Recognitions

  • 2023: UN-Habitat Urban Leader Award
  • 2021: Transparency Kenya Integrity Shield
  • 2020: COTU "Best Labour MP"
  • 2019: EACC Anti-Corruption Champion
